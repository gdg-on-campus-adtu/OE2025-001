`->` A branch is usually a `temporary diversion` that retains the `history` that
leads to it.

<!-- pause -->

`->` Hence, branches can and often are `merged back` into the chain or
`initial branch` they originated from.

Benefits?

<!-- new_line -->
<!-- incremental_lists: true -->

- You can `independently` divide work between numerous `focused branches` in
  parallel.
- Makes `code review` relatively simpler.
- Branches power `GitHub`'s and other forge's `PR` or `Pull Request` system.
